Quart 3.0: Receptionist App-Front End Sub-Lead

Herald, our receptionist check-in web application, is a redesigned check-in web site that allows customers to easily and seamlessly check-in to their appointment without the hassle of dealing with a receptionist. We realize that too many customers are getting tire of filling out a physical form just to check in only to be delayed by the person he or she is trying to make an appointment with. By taking advantage of this niche needs, our application hopes to make this experinece as convenient as possible.


In a team of 15 people, I helped redesigned Herald to better meet the needs and expectations of the customers in during my 4th year at UC San Diego. We realize that the prior year's design of the receptionist application was lacking in user interfaces and online communication channel integration like slack. We incorporated feedback from a mock customer to better understand what the users want and came up with a cleaner landing page, slack integration, and the ability for the owenr of the site to better analyze the information about various companies using the check-in web application.
